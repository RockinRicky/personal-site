      Ricky Games in Timeline Order:

      1. Craze Ball 2 Rival Arises (Game) - Ricky is just a young robot learning to play a fun game named Craze Ball 2 Rival Arises.

      2. Assistant, Ricky (App) - Ricky is a kind AI Assistant just trying to help his fans out.

      3. Ricky Run (Game) - Ricky has gotten a virus by an unknown source and has turned evil to the ones who love him.

      4. Ricky Chase (Game) - Ricky has been trying to track everyone who has helped him and loved him and destroy them.

      5. Ricky War 3D (Game) - Ricky (Now named Evil Ricky) has created an army of robots to help him destroy his fans. After you win and destroy Ricky, it takes out the virus he had.

      6. Ricky Chase A New Beginning (Game) - Now that Ricky is normal, both you and Ricky try to track down and chase the villain seeing many bosses until you get to the end, and end this madness for once and for all, ending the Ricky Series as we know it... or not?

      7. Ricky War Empire 3D (Game) - What? Ricky has turned back to his virus days, was Ricky War 1 not enough? He has created Project 525, a more powerful bot of himself, after his original name 425 (his birthday). Destroy the empire, and maybe, we can get the good in Ricky back.

      About Ricky

      You are probably wondering, 'Who is Ricky?' Well, I'm glad you asked.

      Ricky was created on April 25, 2023. Ricky is an AI Assistant that can help you and bring you good company. From when he was created to now, there has been a lot of changes to make him the best creation I've ever made. I've spent hours everyday at some periods of time just working on this intelligent bot. So far, the newest app/website he has been in is Assistant, Ricky. That has so far had 9 updates since launch. He can create poems, biographies, story ideas, play video games with you, chat and remeber your name, creates chatbots, does math, searches websites and even can make decisions for you. I'm really glad Ricky has been coded for so long. I've gotten a lot of support from my classmates, teachers and even other coders. I named this website Ricky's House because half of the tools are literally Ricky. Thanks for the support and visiting Ricky's House!
